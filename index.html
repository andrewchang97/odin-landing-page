Embark on your cave exploring adventure!

Spelunky 2 builds upon the unique, randomized challenges that made the original a roguelike classic, offering a huge adventure designed to satisfy players old and new. Meet the next generation of explorers as they find themselves on the Moon, searching for treasure and missing family.

Meet some of the explorers!

Ana Spelunky

She's come to the moon to find her mom and dad. An easygoing young lady with the heart of a hero.

Margaret Tunnel

A fascination with the ocean led her away from the family business. Her dream is to buy her own pirate ship.

Colin Northward

An inquisitive artist and engineer who's obsessed with Olmec and the Curse of the Caves.

Roffy D. Sloth

He has a lot of deep thoughts, but not a lot that he wants to say out loud.
